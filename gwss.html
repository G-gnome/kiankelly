Glassy-winged Sharpshooter

Homalodisca vitripennis , aka the glassy-winged sharpshooter, is a xylem feeding leafhopper, invasive to California and an important agricultural pest as a vector of Xylella fastidiosa . For part of my postdoc work with Dr. Jason Stajich, I am working on genome improvements, as well as investigating genomic mechanisms that might lead to insecticide resistance.
